What is Atlas?

Atlas is the central hub for running the Sliderobes business. It brings together order management, purchasing, stock control, accounting, production workflow, scheduling, IT asset tracking, and reporting — all in one place. It integrates directly with Anthill (CRM), Xero (accounting), Microsoft 365, and our internal CAD and manufacturing databases so that data flows seamlessly across every part of the business without manual duplication.
