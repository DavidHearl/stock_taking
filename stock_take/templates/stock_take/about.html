What is Atlas?

Atlas is the central hub for running the Sliderobes business. It brings together order management, purchasing, stock control, accounting, scheduling, reporting, and more — all in one place. It integrates directly with Anthill (CRM), Xero (accounting), and our internal CAD and manufacturing databases so that data flows seamlessly across every part of the business without manual duplication.

Pages & Features

Dashboard

A live overview of key business metrics — sales performance, stock levels, outstanding orders, and monthly trends. Includes downloadable PDF reports for weekly, monthly, and average breakdowns.

Projects

The core of daily operations. Manage orders from sale through to installation. Tracks sales, remedials, customers, events, and contacts — all synced with Anthill.

Accounting

Handles both sales and purchase invoices. Invoices can be raised manually or synced from Xero. Tracks payment status, links invoices to purchase orders, and supports two-way Xero integration.

Purchase

Manage supplier purchase orders from creation to receipt. Supports PDF generation, email dispatch, supplier contacts, and product allocations. Includes Boards Summary and OS Doors Summary views.

Products & Stock

Full stock management including a live stock list, scheduled stock takes, and product substitutions. The shortages view highlights materials at risk of running out across both standard and Raumplus stock.

Calendar

Scheduling hub for fitter appointments and manufacturing. Includes a weekly calendar, fitter timesheets with approval workflows, and a workflow board for tracking jobs through production stages.

Tools

Utility pages for specialist tasks. The Map shows customer and job locations geographically. The Order Generator processes CAD data into material cut lists. Database Check validates data integrity.

Reports

Detailed analytical reports covering material usage, job profitability (costing), and after-sales remedial activity — each with filterable data and PDF export.

Tickets

Internal support ticket system. Staff can raise issues or requests which are tracked through to resolution. Admins see unread counts and open ticket totals directly in the navigation.

Claim Service

Manages claim and warranty documentation. Upload, organise, and download claim files grouped by reference. Supports bulk ZIP downloads for efficient document handling and external submission.

Admin

System administration tools for authorised users. Manage user accounts and role-based permissions, configure system settings, run API scripts against live data, and review the full activity audit log.
